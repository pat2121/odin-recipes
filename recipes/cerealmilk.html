cereal and milk recipe

great cereal and milk please make it

Ingredients

  • 1 part cereal of your choice
  • 1 part milk of your choice

Directions

  1. gather your ingredients and a bowl that is the correct size to hold the amount of food necessary to quell your hunger
  2. pour the cereal of your choice into the bowl
  3. add a portion of milk similar in volume to that of the cereal into the bowl
  4. add any optional toppings of your choice and enjoy!
